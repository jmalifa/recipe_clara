Pepper-chilli beef grill with carrot salad.

Cook this mouth watering pepper-chilli beef grill recipe with carrot salad in under 30-minues

Ingredients
Nutrition

- Coles grill butteflied beef with pepper and chilli.

Carrot Salad

- 4 carrots, peeled, shredded

- 1/4 wombak, finely shredded

1 bunch coriander, leaves picked

- 4 spring onions, shredded

- 1/3 cup Paul Newmans's own classic asian dressing

Nutrional info goes here......

Method
Notes

Step 1

Preheat chargrill or barbecue on high. cook beef according to packet directions. Remove from heat and rest for 10 mins.

Step 2

Meanwhile, combine carrot, wombak, spring onions and coriander leaves in a large bowl. Add dressing and toss to combine.

Step 3

Thickly slice beef. Serve with carrot salad.
